Multi-Experiment Coverage Information Help Page

The multi-experiment coverage page displays significant regulatory effect activity, linking regulatory elements with genes in an interactive chromosome view, integrating data from multiple experiments.

On the multi-experiment page you can:

    View experiment regulatory effects in an interactive chromosome view.
  1. Hover and click highlighted regulatory active regions in chromosome
    2. Compare your data with experiment regulatory activity data:
  2. Use your .bed file to interactively view your data alongside experiment data in the chromosome view.
    3. Easily download all experiment regulatory activity.
  3. Click download button to download data
    4. Download regulatory activity data from specific regions of choice with ease.
  4. Use your .bed file to download experiments region data.
