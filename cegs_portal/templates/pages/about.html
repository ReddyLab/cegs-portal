Duke Center for Combinitorial Gene Regulation Data Portal

What is the Duke CCGR?

The goal of the Duke Center for Combinitorial Gene Regulation (CCGR) is to make combinatorial studies of the function of regulatory elements and noncoding variants routine.

What is the CCGR Portal?

The CCGR data portal is where experiment results from the CCGR and other sources, such as ENCODE, are loaded for dissemination, exploration, and further analysis.

What You Can Do with the CCGR Portal

  • Explore genomic regions
  • Discover and download results from experiments performed on your favorite gene
  • Look at multiple experiment result sets at the same time
  • Find effects associated with elements in a specific region
